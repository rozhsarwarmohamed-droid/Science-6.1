The Size Illusion 🔭

The Sun is HUGE. The Moon is small. But they look the same size from Earth! Why?

☀️ The Sun – CLOSE 🌑 The Moon – FAR
📏 Move the Slider – Watch what happens!

🤯 Amazing!
The Sun is HUGE, but it is very far away!
The Moon is small, but it is very close!
That is why they look the same size! 🌓
🌟 Key Idea: Distance changes how big things look. A big thing far away can look the same as a small thing close up!

Gravity is a pulling force. It pulls everything towards the centre!

🌍 Gravity pulls towards the centre!
